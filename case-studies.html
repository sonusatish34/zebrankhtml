Case Studies

Over the years, Zebrank Software Solutions has delivered countless innovative solutions to industry leaders across various sectors, including E-commerce, Hospitality, Healthcare, and more. Explore why startups and Fortune 1000 companies trust us to turn their visions into reality with cutting-edge technology and seamless user experiences.

Dozzy is an innovative online platform designed to simplify the farmhouse booking process. It offers customers the ability to discover and book unique, comfortable farmhouse stays, catering to individuals and families who want to experience an escape in nature. The client approached Zebrank Software Solutions to build an intuitive and scalable platform that would allow users to browse, book, and manage farmhouse reservations easily, both on their phones and computers.

This case study highlights how Zebrank Software Solutions successfully delivered a comprehensive, scalable, and user-friendly platform for Dozzy, enabling them to effectively meet the demands of both their customers and internal teams.

LongDrive Cars is a fast-growing car rental company that offers an easy-to-use platform for booking vehicles. Whether for a day trip or a long vacation, customers can browse and reserve cars effortlessly. To support their expansion, the company required a scalable app and website to streamline bookings and operations across multiple locations.

Zebrank Software Solutions developed a comprehensive digital platform that simplified the rental process and improved operational efficiency.
