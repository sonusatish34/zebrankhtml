Quality Assurance & Engineering

At Zebrank, we believe that the quality of your software is essential to its success. Our Quality Assurance (QA) and Engineering services ensure that your applications meet the highest standards of performance, security, and user satisfaction. We provide a comprehensive range of testing solutions, from manual testing to automated testing, that help you identify and fix issues before they affect your end-users. With a focus on precision, reliability, and scalability, we ensure your software is robust and ready for the market.

Manual Testing

Despite the rise of automation, manual testing remains crucial for identifying issues that may not be caught by automated scripts. Our team of experienced QA professionals performs in-depth manual testing to evaluate your application’s functionality, usability, and user experience. We test your application across different devices, browsers, and operating systems to ensure it performs flawlessly for all users.

Automated Testing

Automated testing allows for faster, more efficient testing cycles, especially for repetitive tasks. Zebrank offers robust automated testing services using popular tools like Selenium, Appium, and JUnit. Our team builds and executes automated test scripts to ensure your application’s functionality is thoroughly verified while reducing the time and cost involved in manual testing.

Performance Testing

Ensuring your application performs well under varying loads and conditions is critical to providing a positive user experience. Our performance testing services evaluate your application’s speed, scalability, and reliability under heavy traffic and stress. We simulate different user scenarios to identify potential bottlenecks, memory leaks, and performance degradation, and provide recommendations for optimization.

Security Testing

Security is a top priority for any application. Zebrank’s security testing services ensure that your application is protected from potential vulnerabilities, data breaches, and cyber threats. We conduct thorough penetration testing, vulnerability scanning, and security audits to identify weaknesses in your app and fix them before they can be exploited. Our security tests ensure that your software is safe for users and compliant with industry standards.

Functional Testing

Functional testing is aimed at ensuring that your software works as expected and meets the specified requirements. Our team meticulously tests every feature, function, and workflow of your application, ensuring that it delivers the promised outcomes. We verify that all user interactions, from logging in to performing key tasks, are working as intended.

Regression Testing

When changes are made to your software, it’s essential to ensure that existing functionality remains unaffected. Zebrank offers regression testing to validate that new updates or bug fixes do not break existing features. This type of testing helps us ensure that every version of your application maintains the quality and functionality of previous releases.

Usability Testing

The user experience is paramount to your software’s success. Our usability testing services ensure that your application is intuitive, easy to use, and meets the needs of your target audience. We observe real users interacting with your app, gather feedback, and recommend changes to improve the overall user experience, design, and usability.

Mobile App Testing

With mobile usage continuing to rise, ensuring your mobile application is free from bugs and performs well on a wide range of devices is essential. Zebrank offers mobile app testing services for both iOS and Android platforms. We test your mobile application for functionality, performance, compatibility, and security to provide a seamless experience across different devices and operating systems.

Test Automation Framework Development

To streamline and optimize the testing process, we design and develop custom test automation frameworks tailored to your application. These frameworks help ensure that automated tests are reusable, scalable, and maintainable, making your software development lifecycle more efficient and effective.

Continuous Integration & Continuous Testing (CI/CT)

To keep up with fast-paced development cycles, Zebrank implements Continuous Integration and Continuous Testing (CI/CT) practices to ensure that testing happens automatically whenever new code is integrated. This enables real-time feedback on the quality of the code and helps catch issues early in the development process, ultimately speeding up time-to-market and reducing the risk of defects.
